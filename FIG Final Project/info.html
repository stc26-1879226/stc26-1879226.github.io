Sam: Public Parks and Green Spaces

Background

Historically, Seattle has not been known for its expansive view of the sky or city blocks lined with greenery. However, in recent times, the local government has made quite the effort to start improving community access to parks and greenery.

Observations

Change has been noticeable, and the community has responded well to the change within the confines of the Seattle city blocks. On most sidewalks you can look left and right and be pleasantly surprised to find trees and plants. Some portions of the city have even been paved around a single, fully grown evergreen tree!
